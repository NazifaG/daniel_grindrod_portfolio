Historical Consulting

Media


I have over a decade's experience in making scholarly
historical research accessible to a wide range of audiences.

My research expertise lies in late medieval and early modern
Italy but I also have experience working on much broader
periods of history. I can advise on a wide range of periods,
particularly on social history.



Archival Research



I am available to undertake bespoke archival
research in the UK and Italy. My expertise is in
documents from the fourteenth to seventeenth
centuries in English and Italian languages.

I am based in Edinburgh so have convenient
access to numerous Scottish archives and those
in the rest of the UK. I regularly visit Italy to
undertake research and can make additional
trips to the Italian archives by arrangement.

Please contact me if you have an archival
project you'd like to discuss.
